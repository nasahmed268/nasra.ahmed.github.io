SEO & SMO Techniques 📈💬

SEO Techniques 🔍

Meta tags are very important for search engine performance because they give search engines information about a web page. By getting people to click through to the site, meta descriptions help search engines better understand the material and make the site more visible (Digital Marketing Group, 2024).

- Using important keywords in page names can help your search engine ranks, which makes it easier for people to find your content. Digital Marketing Group (2024) says that each page should have its own title to keep things clear and give users the best experience possible.

- Using heading tags like <h1> and <h2> is important for organizing material and making it easy to find. Digital Marketing Group (2024) says that they also help search engines understand the order of information, which is good for SEO.

SMO Techniques 📲💡

Visitors are more likely to share content when you add social media sharing buttons like the Instagram and Facebook buttons (Kenton, 2023). This increases user involvement and site exposure.

Having an official Instagram account linked to your website can help people trust you and make it easy for them to keep up with your posts. According to Investopedia (Kenton, 2023), this straight link encourages openness and trust.

Putting a "Like" button on your website makes it easy for people to share and support your content. According to Investopedia (Kenton, 2023), this can increase business and make people more aware of your brand.
